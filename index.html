Floor Work Outs For Home

By Tamara Snogren

Introduction

Due to COVID, many gyms have temporarily closed. We provide a list of Floor Work Out plans that you can do from your living room.

Plank

  • Targets: Transverse Abdominis, Internal/External obliques, scapular, rectus abdominis
  • Begin with Push Up position; Keep body Flat and elevated place forearms on the floor
  • Engage your muscles and hold position for 1 minute

Bicycle Crunch

  • Targets: abdominal and oblique
  • Bend knees while back is flat on floor
  • Place hands on back of head and interlock fingers while keeping elbows wide
  • Bring knees up, straighten right leg like riding bike, turn upper body left while bringing right elbow to left knee
  • Repeat to other side. Peform 3 sets 15-25 reps
    • <30 seconds

Russian Twist

  • Sit on the floor with knees up and feet slight lifted off of the ground
  • Keep arms towards center of your body. You may hold onto a small weight
  • Rotate upper body from side to side in a half circular motion
  • Make sure to keep elevated whole time
  • Do exercise for 30 seconds, 3 Rep

Review:

  • Plank
  • Crunch
  • Twist
